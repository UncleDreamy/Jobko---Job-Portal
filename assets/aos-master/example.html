Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using JOBKO. Can't find your answer? Contact our support team!

Is JOBKO free to use? ‚ñº

Yes! JOBKO is completely free for job seekers. You can create a profile, browse jobs, and apply to unlimited positions at no cost. Employers have free and premium posting options available.

How do I create an account? ‚ñº

Simply click "Sign Up" in the navigation, choose whether you're a job seeker or employer, and fill in your details. You can also sign up using your Google or LinkedIn account for faster registration.

What types of jobs are available? ‚ñº

JOBKO features both full-time and part-time positions across all industries including tech, design, marketing, healthcare, education, finance, and more. We have remote, hybrid, and on-site opportunities.

How long does it take to get hired? ‚ñº

The hiring timeline varies by company and role. Some positions receive responses within 24-48 hours, while others may take 1-2 weeks. You'll receive notifications when employers review your application.

Can I post multiple jobs as an employer? ‚ñº

Absolutely! Employers can post unlimited job listings. We offer various packages to help you reach more candidates and fill positions faster. Contact our sales team for enterprise solutions.

Is my personal information secure? ‚ñº

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your personal information is never shared without your consent, and you have full control over your profile visibility.
